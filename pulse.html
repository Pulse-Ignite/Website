Pulse

A Free Health & Research Platform

Key Features

Health and Safety

Have all your health information in one place, track your wellness journey and medications, and have access to First Aid guides.

Research Database

Access curated health research and studies. Students and researchers also have access to tools and resources to help with the research process.

PulseBot

Talk to PulseBot, an advanced AI model that can diagnose many common illnesses based on your health profile, past illnesses, and symptoms, and guides you on your health journey.

Pulse Ignite

As the official Pulse Ignite app, Pulse gives you access to many different events, programs, resources, and tools to take your health and research to the next level.
